Why Aurea Mellis Honey Is Different

Commercial honey is heated to 80°C for ease of processing, destroying every enzyme, vitamin, and aromatic compound that makes raw honey extraordinary. We have never heated a single jar above 35°C.

Each batch receives a full biochemical analysis, and the results are published on a per-batch basis. Nothing is withheld. Nothing is averaged.

  • Diastase activity verified at ≥ 15 DN per batch
  • HMF (hydroxymethylfurfural) below 10mg/kg in all reserves
  • Moisture content 17–19% — the natural optimal range
  • Pollen spectrum mapped to genus level by independent lab
  • No pesticide residues — all apiaries verified annually
  • Zero additives, zero preservatives, zero sugar additions

Every jar is numbered. Every harvest is unrepeatable. Reserve yours before the season closes.

The Discipline

An Ancient
Precision Science

The sky at the moment of your birth is not metaphor — it is cartography. At CAELUM HOUSE, our master astrologers read natal charts with the rigour of scholars and the intuition of artists.

I

The Natal Chart

Your birth chart is a singular cosmological document — a precise record of the sky's configuration at the exact moment of your arrival. It maps the positions of all classical and modern planets, the twelve houses, and the mathematical angles that define your archetypal makeup.

Foundation Reading
II

Transits & Timing

The planets never stand still. Transit analysis overlays the current sky upon your natal chart, revealing the cyclical tides of influence shaping your present moment and near future. We identify critical windows — for decision-making, for creative work, for essential stillness.

Predictive Reading
III

Synastry

In synastry, two birth charts are superimposed. The aspects formed between them reveal the nature of a relationship — its tensions, harmonies, blind spots, and gifts. We analyse romantic partnerships, familial bonds, and professional alliances with equal rigour and discretion.

Relational Analysis

How It Works

The Reading
Process

I

Submit Your Birth Data

You provide your full name, date of birth, exact time of birth, and city of birth. Precision is essential — a few minutes' difference in birth time can shift house cusps significantly. We handle all data with complete discretion, under Swiss privacy standards.

At Time of Inquiry
II

Chart Calculation & Preliminary Study

Your astrologer calculates the natal chart using the Placidus house system as primary, cross-referenced against Whole Signs and Campanus. All major and minor aspects are logged. The astrologer spends a minimum of four hours in solitary study before any session.

48–72 Hours Preparation
III

Private Consultation Session

The session is conducted in-person at one of our atelier locations or via encrypted private video. Your astrologer presents their findings, addresses your questions, and — critically — listens. The chart is not a monologue; it is a dialogue between the document and your lived experience.

90–120 Min Session
IV

Written Report

Following the session, your astrologer composes a written document of fifteen to twenty-five pages — not a template, but a bespoke analysis written specifically for you. It includes your annotated chart, interpretive commentary, and timing notes for the year ahead. Delivered within five business days.

5 Business Days Delivery
V

Follow-Up Access

Clients retain thirty days of follow-up access to their astrologer via encrypted correspondence. Questions arising from the report, clarifications on timing windows, or observations from your own life that illuminate the chart are all welcome within this period.

30-Day Access Window

Readings

Consultation Types

Six Disciplines, One Standard

Each reading type at Caelum House represents a distinct astrological discipline. We do not offer all things to all clients. We offer six carefully defined consultation types — each requiring a minimum preparation period and a minimum appointment duration.

Foundation
Natal Chart Reading
90 min · First Consultation

A comprehensive interpretation of the natal chart — planets, houses, aspects, and their interaction. The essential foundation of any astrological understanding.

Annual
Solar Return Reading
75 min · Annual

The chart of the moment the sun returns to its natal position — the astrological portrait of your year ahead. Profoundly useful for planning and understanding cycles.

Relationship
Synastry & Composite
90 min · Two Charts

The astrological examination of two charts in relationship — synastry for interpersonal dynamics, composite for the entity formed by two individuals together.

Classical
Jyotish Reading
90 min · Vedic System

A reading in the Jyotish tradition — the sidereal zodiac, the Nakshatras, and the Vimshottari dasha system. Offered by our specialist Jyotish practitioners only.

Predictive
Transits & Progressions
60 min · Follow-up

Current planetary transits and progressed chart positions in dialogue with the natal chart — what is moving, when, and what it may indicate for the months ahead.

Extended
Deep Chart Work
3 hrs across 2 sessions

For clients who wish to engage with their chart in sustained depth — a two-session extended consultation covering natal, predictive, and life-phase themes.

Our Standard

Preparation Before
Every Consultation

Before any appointment at Caelum House, your practitioner prepares your chart in full — typically three to four hours of analysis, completed in advance of the session. We do not read charts cold in the consultation room.

This preparation is non-negotiable and is reflected in the lead time required for bookings: a minimum of ten working days for all first consultations.

How We Work

The Process

From Enquiry to Interpretation

At Caelum House, a consultation is not a performance. It is the culmination of significant preparatory work — analysis completed before you arrive, so that the session itself can be a genuine conversation rather than a calculation.

I
Enquiry & Matching

Your enquiry is reviewed by our practice coordinator, who considers your stated purpose and assigns you to the most appropriate practitioner based on their specialisation, availability, and the type of reading requested.

II
Birth Data Collection

Exact birth date, time, and location — the three coordinates from which everything follows. We accept data from birth certificates only; approximate birth times are noted and addressed in the reading with appropriate caveats.

III
Chart Preparation

The practitioner casts the chart and prepares a full written analysis — typically three to four hours of preparatory work. No consultation begins without this preparation completed. The lead time of ten working days exists to protect this phase.

IV
The Consultation

A private session of ninety minutes or more, conducted in-person at one of our consultation rooms or via secure video call. The practitioner shares their analysis, listens to your responses, and adjusts the interpretation accordingly. It is a dialogue, not a monologue.

V
Written Summary

Within five working days of the consultation, the practitioner provides a written summary of the key themes discussed — not a transcript, but a considered document of the most significant points. Retained in your file for future reference.

Our Team

Culinary Masters

The visionary minds behind every unforgettable course

Laurent Dubois

Executive Chef

Trained at Le Cordon Bleu in Paris and honed at three-star Michelin kitchens across France and Japan. With over 20 years of experience, Chef Dubois blends classical French technique with modern sensibility.

Amara Singh

Pastry Chef

A pioneer of avant-garde desserts, Amara transforms seasonal fruits and rare chocolates into sculptural works of art. Her creations have been featured in Bon Appetit and Food & Wine.

Marcus Chen

Head Sommelier

Certified Master Sommelier and curator of our 15,000-bottle cellar. Marcus sources exceptional vintages from Burgundy, Piedmont, Napa Valley, and emerging wine regions across the globe.

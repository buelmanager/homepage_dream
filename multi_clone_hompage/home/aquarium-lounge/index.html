Featured

Marine Species

Meet the remarkable inhabitants of ABYSSAL. Each species tells a story of evolution, adaptation, and the fragile beauty of ocean life.

Least Concern

Giant Pacific Octopus

Enteroctopus dofleini

The largest octopus species, renowned for extraordinary intelligence and problem-solving abilities. Can change color and texture in milliseconds.

Least Concern

Blue Tang Collection

Paracanthurus hepatus

Vibrant royal blue surgeonfish found in coral reefs of the Indo-Pacific. Recognized worldwide for their striking coloration and schooling behavior.

Vulnerable

Hammerhead Sharks

Sphyrna mokarran

Apex predators with their iconic cephalofoil providing 360-degree vision. Our conservation program supports wild population recovery efforts.

Not Evaluated

Moon Jellyfish

Aurelia aurita

Translucent bells pulsing with ethereal grace. These ancient organisms have drifted through oceans for over 500 million years.

Since 2009, ABYSSAL has been at the forefront of marine conservation. Our dedicated team of marine biologists works tirelessly to protect endangered species, rehabilitate injured marine animals, and educate the public about ocean stewardship.

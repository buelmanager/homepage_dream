Our Story

About

The Atelier Creed

"Every origin tells a story the tongue already knows but the mind has not yet named."

Umbra Cacao was born from the conviction that industrial chocolate had severed the thread connecting the bean to the bar. We exist to restore that thread — through strict single-origin sourcing, minimal processing, and a small-batch discipline that limits us to seven origin bars each year. Each release is a portrait: a particular altitude, a particular fermentation, a particular farmer's hands.

The Founder

Rooted in the Shadow of the Bean

Elena Vasquez spent the first decade of her career as a cocoa agronomist, working with small-hold farmers across Peru, Madagascar, and São Tomé. Where others saw crop yield data, she perceived flavour complexity: the wet-process notes from a valley microclimate, the violet finish of a shade-grown Criollo, the tannic spine of a Forastero grown at 1,400 metres.

In 2014, after three years sourcing for a Zürich confectionery house, she founded Umbra Cacao — "shadow cacao" in homage to the shade-grown trees that define the finest beans. The atelier operates on a single principle: source a bean remarkable enough to eat without augmentation, then refine the least possible process to reveal what is already there.

Today Umbra accepts applications for seven annual commissions. Each origin is released once, in a limited edition of 240 bars, each numbered and signed.

Core Principles

What We Stand For

01
Single Origin, Unblended

Every bar is made from beans of a single farm, in a single harvest year. No blending, no correction with secondary origins. The terroir speaks without mediation.

02
Minimal Process

We use two ingredients: cacao and unrefined cane sugar. Nothing is added to alter, mask, or elevate the base flavour. Roast profile, conch time, and grind are adjusted to reveal, not manufacture.

03
Farmer Partnership

Our sourcing partners receive above-market premiums and co-authorship credit on every bar. Their name, altitude, and fermentation notes are printed on the wrapper alongside ours.

The Range

Six shape families.
Infinite variations.

Pellucid does not produce boards for stock. Every board shown here represents a shape family — a consistent template philosophy — that Marcus will adapt, modify, or completely reimagine based on your consultation. These are reference points, not catalogues. Lead times are currently 8 to 14 weeks depending on material and complexity.

The Standard

Every board is held to the same measure

Pellucid does not have a tier system. Every board, whether a PU fish or a balsa longboard, receives the same amount of time, attention, and material care. Marcus will not release a board he considers unfinished, regardless of the delivery date.

  • All shapes planed and sanded by hand — no machine finishing
  • Glass schedule specified per board based on rider weight and wave type
  • All fin boxes set by hand with alignment checked before glassing
  • Leash plug installed after glassing, not before
  • Every board measured against the original template at completion
  • Delivery includes a written build record stating all materials and dimensions

Ready to start?

Every commission begins with a consultation. Tell us about your wave, your riding style, and what your current board does not do. We will take it from there.

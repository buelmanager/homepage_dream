About

The Philosophy

"A surfboard is not manufactured. It is argued with, negotiated, and eventually resolved."

Pellucid makes custom surfboards for surfers who know what they want — and for surfers who do not yet know how to ask for it. The process begins with a conversation. It ends with a single board made for one person's relationship with one kind of wave. We have never made the same board twice.

The Shaper

Marcus
Vidal-
Serrano

Marcus grew up in the water between Tarifa and Tarragona, learning to read waves before he learned to shape them. His first board came apart on the second session. The second one lasted two years. He shaped his first commercial board in 2004, working out of a rented garage in Málaga.

By 2008 he had refined the process to something he was willing to put his name on, and Pellucid was established — not as a brand, but as a working principle. Every board he makes is the result of a minimum of two hours in conversation with the person who will ride it. He does not take orders by email. He does not quote from a price list. Every engagement begins at zero.

Marcus currently shapes approximately 40 boards per year, working alone. He employs no assistants. He accepts no commissions he cannot personally complete within a single shaping session.

Working Principles

What Pellucid
stands for

01

One Shaper, One Board

Every Pellucid board is shaped, glassed, and finished by Marcus alone. No production staff, no manufacturing floor, no templates used without modification. If Marcus cannot make your board, Pellucid cannot make your board.

02

The Wave Comes First

Pellucid boards are shaped for specific conditions, not for a rider's general preference. If you ride a beach break in summer and a reef in winter, you need two boards. We will tell you honestly when what you are describing will not work on your break.

03

No Rush Orders

The current lead time is 8 to 14 weeks depending on material and complexity. This is not a service failure. It is a structural commitment: every board receives the same amount of time, regardless of when it was ordered. There is no queue-jumping.

History

How we got here

2004

The First Garage

Marcus begins shaping in a rented garage in Málaga. First year output: 11 boards, all for local surfers. No website, no social media, word of mouth only. Three boards are returned for fin box corrections. He fixes them and learns to measure twice.

2008

Pellucid Established

After four years of commercial shaping, Marcus formalises the practice under the name Pellucid — from the Latin for transparent, clear, unobstructed. The name reflects his approach to client conversation: nothing is assumed, everything is stated. A purpose-built shaping bay is constructed beside his home workshop.

2015

Materials Expansion

Following a visit to a Japanese wood workshop, Marcus begins incorporating hand-selected balsa and paulownia into the material palette. The first all-wood board is made for a longboard surfer in Hossegor and is still in active use. The wood programme remains a small part of the overall output — approximately six boards per year.

2022

The Consultation System

Marcus formalises the pre-commission consultation as a structured two-hour session, always conducted in person at the workshop or at the client's home break. The session is free. It results either in a commission or in a clear explanation of why Pellucid is not the right fit for the client's requirements. Both outcomes are considered successful.

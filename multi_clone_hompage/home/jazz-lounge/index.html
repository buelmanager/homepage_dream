The Experience

More Than a Venue

An underground sanctuary for music lovers. Six distinct experiences, one unforgettable night.

🎷

Live Performance

World-class jazz musicians take our intimate stage every night. From solo piano to full quintets, experience music the way it was meant to be heard — close enough to feel every note.

🎶

Vinyl Listening

Over 3,000 rare vinyl records. Custom-built tube amplifiers and handmade speakers. Choose an album, sink into a leather chair, and rediscover analog warmth.

🍸

Cocktail Bar

Jazz-inspired craft cocktails by our award-winning mixologist. Each drink tells a story — from the smoky "Coltrane" to the bright "Bird Lives." Premium whiskeys and rare spirits.

🎙

Private Sessions

Reserve our velvet-lined listening rooms for private gatherings. Curated playlists, personal bartender, and our finest sound system. Perfect for celebrations and intimate evenings.

🎹

Jazz Workshops

Learn from the masters. Monthly workshops cover improvisation, music theory, and instrument technique. All skill levels welcome — from first-timers to seasoned players.

🌙

Late Night Jams

After midnight, the stage opens up. Local musicians, visiting artists, and brave amateurs converge for spontaneous jam sessions. Some of our most legendary moments happen after hours.

FAQ

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before your first visit to the Blue Note underground.

Do I need a reservation?

+
For weekend performances (Friday and Saturday), we strongly recommend reservations as we often reach capacity. Weeknight shows are generally walk-in friendly, but reserving guarantees your preferred seating. You can book through our website, by phone, or via the concierge desk. Vinyl listening sessions are always first-come, first-served.

Is there a dress code?

+
We maintain a smart casual atmosphere. Think dark jeans, collared shirts, cocktail attire, or your favorite vintage look. No athletic wear or flip-flops, please. Jazz has always been about expressing yourself — dress the part and feel the vibe.

Can I bring my instrument for the jam sessions?

+
Absolutely! Our late-night jam sessions (typically after midnight on Fridays) welcome all musicians. We provide a house drum kit, upright bass, and piano. Bring your horn, guitar, or voice and sign up with the bandleader. All skill levels are welcome — the only requirement is respect for the music and your fellow players.

How does the vinyl listening room work?

+
Our listening room houses over 3,000 vinyl records spanning the full history of jazz. Browse the stacks, select an album, and our staff will set it up on one of our three custom turntable stations. Each station has vintage tube amplifiers and hand-built speakers. Sessions run 45 minutes and include a complimentary drink. Available daily from 5 PM.

Can I book the venue for a private event?

+
Yes! We offer private hire for up to 80 guests. Our space is perfect for corporate events, birthday celebrations, album release parties, and intimate gatherings. Packages include a dedicated bartender, curated live music, and custom cocktail menus. Contact us at events@bluenote.club for availability and pricing.

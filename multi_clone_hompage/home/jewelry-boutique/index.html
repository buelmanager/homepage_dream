FAQs

Questions We
Often Hear

We believe informed clients make the most meaningful choices. Here are answers to the questions our clients most frequently ask.

Bespoke commissions typically take 8–16 weeks from initial consultation to delivery. The timeline depends on design complexity, gemstone sourcing, and the intricacy of the setting. We'll provide a precise estimate after your design consultation. Rush commissions may be accommodated for certain designs — please contact us to discuss.
Absolutely. Every diamond we use is Kimberley Process certified and conflict-free. We source gemstones directly from reputable, audited mines and cutters who adhere to fair labor practices. All stones above 0.5ct come with a full provenance report and independent GIA or AGS certification.
Our bespoke engagement rings begin at $4,500 for simple solitaire designs in gold with a high-quality diamond. Price depends on the metal, stone grade, carat weight, and design complexity. We work with clients across a range of budgets and always disclose all costs upfront — no surprises.
Yes — your first design consultation is always complimentary and carries no obligation. We invite you to our atelier or arrange a private video consultation. This session helps us understand your vision, preferences, and budget so we can propose the ideal approach for your piece.
Yes, heirloom restoration and replication is one of our most cherished services. Our master restorers can repair damaged settings, re-tip prongs, replace missing stones, re-plate metalwork, and fully restore antique pieces. We can also create faithful reproductions of heirlooms — preserving their design while replacing worn components.

How We Work

THE PROCESS

Step by Step

FIVE STEPS TO PERMANENT

01
Step One — First Contact

THE ENQUIRY FORM

All new commissions begin with our online enquiry form. We ask for concept description, reference imagery, placement on the body, approximate size, and skin tone photographs. This information allows us to make an informed decision about which of our artists is the best match for your vision — because not all styles suit all hands, and we will not assign you to an artist who cannot execute what you need.

Response time: 3–5 business days. We review all submissions personally.
02
Step Two — Meeting

THE CONSULTATION

Once matched, you meet your assigned artist — in the studio if you are local, via video if you are remote. The consultation is not a sales meeting. It is a design conversation. We discuss your concept in depth, explore placement options, and talk about what the piece means to you. We also assess whether UV-reactive neon inks are appropriate for your skin tone and the intended placement. There is no obligation to proceed.

Duration: 60–90 minutes. Consultation is complimentary for all new clients.
03
Step Three — Creation

THE DESIGN

Following the consultation, the artist creates a fully custom design. We do not send the design for pre-approval. The reveal happens on the day of your session, when the stencil is on your skin and the placement can be evaluated in context. If, at that point, you want adjustments to placement or scale, we make them before starting. If, in the rare case, you are unhappy with the concept, we reschedule at no charge.

Design time: typically 2–8 weeks, depending on complexity.
04
Step Four — The Work

THE SESSION

Sessions are scheduled for 4–8 hours maximum. For large pieces requiring multiple sessions, the schedule is planned at the consultation and confirmed before any work begins. We do not book back-to-back sessions without recovery days between. Our studio plays music but the volume follows the client's preference. Water and snacks are provided. We check in regularly. We never rush.

UV-reactive inks are applied as a final pass, after all base colours are set.
05
Step Five — The Long Game

AFTERCARE & FOLLOW-UP

We provide written aftercare instructions tailored to the specific inks used in your piece. UV-reactive work requires specific sun protection protocol — we cover this in detail. We follow up at 7 days (healing assessment), 21 days (check-in), and 6 weeks (final review). All touch-ups required within 12 months due to healing variance are complimentary. This is our guarantee and our commitment.

Touch-up eligibility: healing-related only. Not applicable to client-requested design changes.
What We Use

THE TOOLS

⚡

UV REACTIVE INKS

Sourced from two certified labs with full allergen screening and long-term biocompatibility studies. Each ink batch is tested in-studio before use. Glow under UV light with zero visible pigment in standard lighting.

◈

HIGH-PIGMENT NEONS

Conventional high-saturation pigment inks in the neon spectrum — pink, cyan, violet, chartreuse. Visible under all lighting conditions. Colour-mixed in-studio for the exact chromatic weight required for each piece's colour architecture.

○

WIRELESS MACHINES

All artists at Voltage Ink use rotary wireless machines for full control over needle depth and speed variation. No coil machines. Wireless eliminates cable tension on the hand, allowing more consistent line work over long sessions.

◻

MEDICAL GRADE PREP

Sterile single-use needles, medical-grade skin prep, and clinical-level surface sanitation between every client. Our infection control protocol exceeds California state requirements and is audited annually by an independent hygiene consultant.

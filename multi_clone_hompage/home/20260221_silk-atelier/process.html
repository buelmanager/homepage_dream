Process

01 / SELECTION

Silk Selection

We select only the finest mulberry silk. Each cocoon is examined for quality before processing.

02 / SPINNING

Spinning

Raw silk is spun into threads of consistent weight. The twist determines the final character.

03 / WEAVING

Weaving

On traditional looms, artisans weave each piece. The rhythm of the shuttle creates patterns only possible by hand.

04 / FINISHING

Finishing

Each piece is washed, pressed, and finished by hand. The final touch is the maker's mark.

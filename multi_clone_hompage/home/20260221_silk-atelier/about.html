About

Our Story

Filament & Reve was born from a simple belief: that true luxury lies in material speaking without noise. We work with the finest silk, chosen not for spectacle but for the way it holds light and memory.

Based in Lyon, our atelier continues traditions dating back centuries. Each piece is a transmission of patience into form.

Material Philosophy

Quality begins at the source

We work only with materials we can trace to their origin — tanneries we have visited, felt mills whose processes we know by name and by hand.

01

Hutteaux Felt, Lyon

Our primary felt supplier since 1948. Rabbit and beaver-blend felts of extraordinary density and resilience.

02

Bernini Sinamay, Como

Italian-woven banana fiber sinamay in weights from gossamer to sculptural. The most responsive material for grand brim work.

03

Leatherworks Breton, Nantes

Full-grain calfskin hat bands and brims. Hand-burnished, edge-painted, unwavering in character.

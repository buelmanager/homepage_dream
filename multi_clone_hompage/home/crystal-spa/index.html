Heritage

Two Decades of
Crystal Mastery

Founded in a small London atelier in 2006, LUMARA was built on a single conviction: that crystalline science could be translated into the language of luxury wellness. Eighteen years later, our legacy is measured not in awards, but in resonances transformed.

2006

The Founding Atelier

LUMARA opens its doors in Marylebone, London — two therapists, six crystals, and a singular vision to unite ancient mineral science with modern luxury wellness.

2010

The Myanmar Expedition

Our founders travel to Mogok, Myanmar to establish direct relationships with ruby and sapphire crystal mines — the beginning of our global provenance program.

2014

Kirlian Photography Protocol

LUMARA becomes the first European spa to integrate Kirlian aura photography as a standard before-and-after diagnostic tool in all treatments.

2018

The Grand Sanctum Opens

Our flagship six-suite Sanctum opens in Knightsbridge. All suites lined with raw amethyst panels from Brazil. Fully booked within 72 hours of announcement.

2024

Global Expansion

LUMARA announces partnerships across 23 countries of crystal origin. Certified specialist training programme launched, accrediting 47 practitioners worldwide.

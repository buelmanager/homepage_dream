About

"A knife is not made — it is revealed. Each blade is a negotiation between metal, heat, and the maker's hands."

— IGNIS LAMA Philosophy

The Fourth Generation

Born into the sparks and heat of the forge, I learned to read steel before I learned to read books. My grandfather's hands guided mine, teaching that the metal has its own will — our job is to listen.

After years of training in Seki, Japan's knife capital, I returned home with new techniques but the same fundamental truth: true craft cannot be rushed. A blade deserves the time it demands.

Today, I continue what began in 1887 — not as a business, but as a calling.

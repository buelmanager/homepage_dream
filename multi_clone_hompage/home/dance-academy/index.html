FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first class at CADENCE.

Do I need dance experience to join?

+
No experience necessary for Beginner classes. We welcome absolute beginners. Our Beginner-level classes like Hip Hop, Jazz Funk, K-Pop, and Stretch & Flow are designed for first-timers. Our instructors break down each move step by step. Many of our students discovered their passion for dance right here at CADENCE.

What should I wear to class?

+
Wear comfortable, form-fitting clothing that allows full range of motion. Leggings or joggers with a fitted top work great. For Ballet, bring ballet slippers; for Hip Hop, clean sneakers; for Contemporary and Jazz, bare feet or half-sole shoes. Avoid jewelry and tie back long hair.

How do memberships work?

+
We offer flexible plans: Drop-In ($25/class), 10-Class Pass ($200, valid 3 months), Monthly Unlimited ($149/month), and Annual Unlimited ($1,290/year). All memberships include access to every class style. First-timers can try a Trial Class for just $15.

Can I attend performances and recitals?

+
Absolutely! CADENCE hosts seasonal showcases every quarter where students perform on our in-house stage. Our annual Winter Gala and Summer Spotlight are highlights of the year. All students are welcome to participate regardless of skill level.

Do you offer private lessons?

+
Yes, private one-on-one lessons are available with all our instructors. Rates start at $75/hour. Private lessons are ideal for wedding first dances, audition preparation, or accelerated skill development. Contact us to schedule.

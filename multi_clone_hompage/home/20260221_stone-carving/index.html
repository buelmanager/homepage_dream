The Atelier

The Art of Revelation

Working exclusively in hand-selected granite, marble, and schist. Each piece begins as a conversation between the stone and the carver, seeking the form that already exists within.

Our studio in Carrara continues traditions spanning millennia. Three generations of carvers work in harmony, each bringing their unique sensitivity to the craft.

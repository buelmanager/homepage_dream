About

Our Legacy

PETRA SOMA — Greek for "Body of Stone" — represents three generations of master carvers in Carrara, Italy.

Every block of stone contains a form waiting to be revealed. We carve not to impose, but to release the shape that already exists within.

Works are accepted by invitation only.

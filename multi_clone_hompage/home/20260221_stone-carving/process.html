Process

01 / SELECTION

Stone Selection

Each block is personally selected from the finest quarries in Carrara. We listen for the sound of the stone — it reveals its character.

02 / STUDY

Form Study

Before touching the stone, we study it for weeks. The form already exists within. Our task is revelation, not imposition.

03 / CHISELING

Rough Carving

Using traditional hand tools, we remove the excess to reveal the form. Each stroke is deliberate.

04 / FINISHING

Refinement

Hours of detailed work bring the surface to its final state. The finish is determined by the stone itself.

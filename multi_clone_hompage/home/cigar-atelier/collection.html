Leaf Selection

The Wrapper Origins

Each wrapper leaf is sourced directly by our master blender, aged a minimum of three years in our Geneva climate-controlled vault before selection. The wrapper is the cigar's face — it communicates before a single note is tasted.

Colorado Oscuro
Nicaragua · Jalapa Valley
Rich, oily, and dense. Carries natural oils that amplify the sweetness of the filler. A dominant wrapper for dominant cigars.
Colorado Claro
Ecuador · Tungurahua Shade
Silken texture, pale amber. Grown under equatorial shade canopies to reduce cell walls and produce unmatched finesse.
Maduro
Connecticut · Broadleaf
Three-year fermented broadleaf from the Connecticut River Valley. Dark, almost black. Concentrated sweetness, zero bitterness.
Cameroon
Cameroon · Centre Region
African earth and dried fruit. The most complex wrapper in our rotation. Highly seasonal — harvested in limited annual lots.
Claro
Cuba · Vuelta Abajo
Pale gold with exceptional smoothness. Rarely available outside Havana. Reserved exclusively for our bespoke commission programme.
Habano 2000
Ecuador · Esmeraldas
Cuban-seed grown at elevation in Ecuador. Spice-forward, aggressive, transformative. The engine behind La Sombra.

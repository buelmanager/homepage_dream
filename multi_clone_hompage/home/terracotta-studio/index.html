The Atelier

Where Art Meets Craft

Our atelier in the heart of the countryside has been a sanctuary for ceramic arts for over two decades. Here, traditional techniques meet contemporary design, creating pieces that are both timeless and modern.

Our Process

From Clay to Art

1

Preparation

Local clay is aged and prepared, ensuring optimal consistency for throwing.

2

Throwing

Each piece is hand-thrown on the potter's wheel, shaped by experienced hands.

3

Drying

Pieces dry slowly under controlled conditions to prevent cracking.

4

Bisque Fire

First firing at 900°C transforms clay into ceramic.

5

Glazing

Hand-applied glazes create unique finishes, then final firing at 1280°C.

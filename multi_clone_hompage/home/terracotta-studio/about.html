Our Story

A Legacy in Clay

Argile Studio was founded in 1999 by master potter Henri Dubois, who sought to revive the ancient art of terracotta in a contemporary context.

What began as a small workshop has grown into one of the most respected ceramic studios in Europe, yet we maintain the same hands-on approach to craft that defined our founding.

Every piece that leaves our atelier carries with it the passion, skill, and tradition of generations of pottery masters.

Tradition

Honoring centuries-old techniques passed down through generations of master potters.

Innovation

Contemporary design meets timeless craft, creating pieces that feel both classic and modern.

Sustainability

Locally-sourced materials and eco-friendly practices guide every step of our process.

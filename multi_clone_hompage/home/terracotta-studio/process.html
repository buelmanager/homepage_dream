Our Process

1

Clay Preparation

Local clay is aged and wedged to achieve the perfect consistency for throwing.

2

Hand Throwing

Each piece is shaped on the potter's wheel by our skilled artisans.

3

Drying

Pieces dry slowly under controlled conditions to prevent cracking.

4

Bisque Fire

First firing at 900°C transforms raw clay into ceramic.

5

Glazing

Hand-applied glazes and final firing at 1280°C create unique finishes.

Our Philosophy

The Art of Olfactory
Storytelling

At SÈVE, we believe fragrance is the most intimate form of self-expression. Each composition is a narrative — a map of emotions, places, and memories distilled into something eternal.

01

Raw Materiality

We source only from trusted growers who share our obsession with purity. Oud from Assam, rose absolute from Grasse, ambergris aged for decades — materiality is our foundation.

02

Time as Ingredient

Great fragrance cannot be rushed. Our macerations age in silence for months. Our perfumers iterate across seasons until the composition reveals its true character.

03

Personal Mythology

Every bespoke commission begins with a deep consultation — your childhood landscapes, your most vivid memories, your aspirational self — transformed into a scent that is irrevocably yours.

The Atelier

A Laboratory of
Sensory Alchemy

"We do not create perfume. We excavate it — from the deepest layers of what it means to be alive."

Nestled in the 6th arrondissement of Paris, our atelier is a sanctuary of concentration and craft. Twenty-two perfumers work in silence among hundreds of raw materials — each one catalogued, tested, and understood intimately before it touches a composition.

The space itself is designed to eliminate distraction: natural stone, diffused northern light, and an air kept at a precise 18°C to preserve the integrity of volatile compounds. Here, time is the most important tool.

The Process

From Source to
Signature

Step 01

The Consultation

A two-hour private session in our atelier. We explore your landscape of memory, emotion, and aspiration through structured olfactory tests and deep conversation. The brief is written in sensation, not words.

Step 02

Material Selection

Our master perfumer selects from over 340 curated ingredients. Each material is evaluated fresh and aged — understanding how time changes its character is essential to lasting compositions.

Step 03

Accord Building

Three to five preliminary accords are developed, aged for a minimum of eight weeks, and evaluated across morning, midday, and evening — fragrance behaves differently in different light.

Step 04

Refinement Sessions

You return to evaluate the accords on your skin. Your feedback — however intuitive — guides successive refinements. This dialogue can span several months until the composition is undeniably right.

Step 05

Final Bottling

Your fragrance is macerated for a final 12 weeks before filtration and cold-pressing into hand-blown glass vessels. A sealed certificate of exclusivity accompanies each commission. The formula remains yours alone.

Our Heritage

Sixteen Years of
Olfactory Mastery

2008

Founding in Paris

Eloise Marchais opens the first SÈVE atelier on Rue de Buci, working with four perfumers and a curated palette of 60 materials.

2012

New York Expansion

A consultation salon opens in the West Village, bringing bespoke fragrance to an American audience for the first time.

2017

Tokyo Atelier

Collaboration with Japanese material specialists opens new access to rare agarwood and hinoki from the Kiso Valley.

2024

Archive Collection

100 of our most celebrated bespoke commissions are re-edited as a limited archive series — each available in an edition of ten.

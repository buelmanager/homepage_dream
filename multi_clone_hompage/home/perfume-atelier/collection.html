Our Standard

Quality Without
Compromise

Every SÈVE fragrance begins with the same uncompromising standard: only the finest raw materials will do. We maintain relationships with growers and harvesters across four continents — evaluating each year's crop before committing to any purchase.

Our concentration standards exceed industry norms: all SÈVE eaux de parfum are formulated at a minimum 28% fragrance concentration — closer to parfum than most industry standards would demand.

SÈVE — Our Story

The Maison

Brand Manifesto

"We believe the most powerful luxury is something you cannot see — only feel, remember, and carry with you wherever you go."

SÈVE was born from a simple conviction: that fragrance, when approached with the seriousness of a fine art, becomes something far more than a product. It becomes a presence — invisible, intimate, and unmistakably personal. We reject the notion that luxury means uniformity. True luxury begins where mass production ends.

Our name — SÈVE, the French word for sap, for the vital essence that flows through living things — is our commitment. Every fragrance we create carries within it the living energy of rare materials, expert hands, and deep listening. We do not produce; we distill. We do not market; we compose.

Founder's Story

Eloise Marchais

"I spent seven years in the perfumery of Grasse before I understood what it means to truly listen — to a material, to a client, to a moment."

Eloise Marchais trained under master perfumer Jean-Claude Delacroix in Grasse before completing studies in organic chemistry at the École de Chimie de Paris. She founded SÈVE in 2008 with a single principle: that bespoke fragrance should feel like portraiture — a discipline requiring the same patience, attentiveness, and technical mastery as any other fine art.

Today, Eloise leads a team of twelve perfumers across three ateliers, each selected for their ability to listen as much as their ability to create. She personally oversees every bespoke commission, ensuring that the final fragrance reflects not just the brief, but the ineffable essence of the person who commissioned it.

— Eloise Marchais, Founder
Core Values

What We Believe

I

Integrity of Material

We never compromise on sourcing. Every ingredient is traceable, ethically obtained, and evaluated across multiple conditions before it enters any composition.

II

Patience as Practice

We refuse shortcuts. Macerations are never rushed. Refinement sessions happen when the composition is ready — not according to a schedule.

III

Radical Exclusivity

Every bespoke formula belongs irrevocably to the person who commissioned it. We maintain no archive of proprietary compositions for resale.

Our Heritage

Sixteen Years of
Living Craft

2008

Founding in Paris

Eloise Marchais opens the first SÈVE atelier at 6 Rue de Buci with four perfumers and sixty curated materials.

2011

First Archive Composition

The first anonymous bespoke commission is exhibited as part of the Paris Scent Festival — establishing SÈVE's critical reputation.

2015

New York Salon Opens

A consultation salon in the West Village brings the SÈVE experience to North America for the first time.

2017

Tokyo Partnership

Collaboration with Hinoki specialist Tanaka Shoten gives SÈVE access to rare Japanese forest materials.

2024

The Archive Collection

One hundred celebrated commissions, with client permission, are re-edited as a limited archive series — each in an edition of ten.

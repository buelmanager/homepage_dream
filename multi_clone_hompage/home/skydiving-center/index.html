Questions

Skydiving FAQ

You must be at least 18 years old to skydive. No maximum age if you're in good health and can meet the physical requirements.

Wear comfortable, snug-fitting clothes. We provide jumpsuits. Closed-toe shoes are required - sneakers or hiking boots work great.

Plan for 4-6 hours for tandem jumps. This includes registration, training, and the actual jump. Plan a full day for certification courses.

Your safety is our priority. We only fly in optimal conditions. If weather doesn't cooperate, we'll reschedule your jump at no extra cost.

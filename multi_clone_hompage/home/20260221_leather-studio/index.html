The Atelier

Made in Our Studio

Every piece is cut, stitched, and finished by hand in our Stockholm atelier. We use only full-grain vegetable-tanned leather, aged for minimum 18 months in Italian tanneries.

No two pieces are alike. Each hide tells its own story through grain and character.

Full-Grain

We source only full-grain vegetable-tanned hides. The strongest part of the hide, with natural grain that develops a unique patina over time.

18+ Months

Aged in Italian tanneries for minimum 18 months. This slow tanning process creates leather of exceptional durability and character.

By Hand

Every hide is selected by hand. We reject more than we accept. Only the finest specimens become VERAK.

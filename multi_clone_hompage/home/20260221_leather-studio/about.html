About

Our Story

VERAK ATELIER was founded on a simple belief: that objects made with care become companions for life.

Based in Stockholm, we work with traditional techniques passed through generations of craftspeople. Every piece begins with the finest full-grain vegetable-tanned leather, sourced from tanneries in Tuscany.

We make fewer things, better. This is not a slogan—it's our operating principle.

◇

Handcrafted

Every piece is cut, stitched, and finished by hand in our Stockholm atelier.

◇

Full-Grain

We use only full-grain vegetable-tanned leather that develops unique character over time.

◇

Timeless

We design for longevity. These pieces outlast their owners.

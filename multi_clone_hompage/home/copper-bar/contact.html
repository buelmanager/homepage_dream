Verdigris operates on a reservation-only basis. We accept a maximum of fourteen guests per evening, across two seatings. The fixed-format experience — six cocktail courses over approximately two and a half hours — cannot be modified after booking. Please note any dietary requirements or alcohol restrictions in your message and we will design alternatives for the affected courses.

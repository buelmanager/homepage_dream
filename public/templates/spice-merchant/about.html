Origin Story

Born in the Spice Quarter

Reza Karimi was raised above a spice market in Isfahan, Iran. The mathematics of blending — which his grandmother conducted by smell alone — became the foundation of what would become SAFFRANIA seventeen years later.

In 2017, after a decade working as a commodity trader in London's food markets, Reza left to do something more precise. He wanted to close the distance between the field where a spice is harvested and the kitchen where it is used. Not through storytelling alone, but through quality so evident it required no explanation.

SAFFRANIA is headquartered in Bermondsey, London, within walking distance of the original Spice Quarter where Reza first understood what a correctly sourced saffron smelled like compared to the adulterated product he had spent years trading.

What We Stand For

Three Principles

01 / TRACEABILITY

We Know Every Field

Every lot of spice we sell can be traced to a specific farm, a specific season, and a specific harvest date. We visit each supplier once per year, minimum. You will never find vague origins on our labels — only coordinates.

02 / QUALITY

Grade or Nothing

We apply independent laboratory grading to every shipment. Saffron is tested against ISO 3632. Pepper is rated for piperine content. We reject roughly thirty percent of what we source. The rest we sell.

03 / RELATIONSHIP

Fair Partnership

We pay above fair-trade minimum on every spice we source. Our relationships with farmers are multi-year contracts — not spot-market transactions. Stability in supply comes from stability in partnership.

Heritage

A History of the Caravan

2017
Founded in Bermondsey

SAFFRANIA opens its first atelier at 12 Bermondsey Street with three spices: saffron from Khorasan, sumac from Gaziantep, and cardamom from Idukki. The first hundred jars sell in three days.

2019
The Twelve Collection

Expansion to twelve spices, each sourced from a single farm with documented GPS coordinates. The Harvest Journal newsletter begins, reaching 4,000 subscribers in its first year.

2022
First Origin Awards

SAFFRANIA's Khorasan saffron receives the Great Taste Award. Two further spices receive recognition. The atelier expands with a dedicated blending room open by appointment.

2024
The Archive Series

Launch of the Twelve Archive — a complete curated set of all twelve spices in a single library box. Limited to 100 sets annually. Now a recurring sellout with a three-month waitlist.

The Collection

Flame Typology

Six Candles. Six Qualities of Light.

Every IGNIS candle is specified by its flame behaviour first, its fragrance second. Each name in the collection refers not to a scent profile but to an atmospheric condition — a quality of light that the candle produces in a room. The fragrances within each typology are chosen to complement the atmosphere, not to define it.

Current Collection
Still · Type I
EMBER
An unwavering flame in pure beeswax. The quality of light this candle produces is that of embers rather than active fire — steady, golden, and free of movement. Fragranced with birchwood, vetiver root, and a trace of cold stone. For rooms of concentrated thought.
220g · 50hr burn · Beeswax · Antwerp
Dancing · Type II
NOCTE
A responsive flame that reads air currents and moves with them. The light this candle produces is inhabited — it suggests that the room is alive. Fragranced with dark amber, smoked oud, and dried rose. The candle for evenings when conversation is the purpose.
220g · 48hr burn · Coconut blend · Antwerp
Anchored · Type III
SOLUM
A flame with a wide, low base and minimal rise — it creates a pool of warm light on the surface beneath it rather than projecting upward. The quality is grounding: it draws attention downward, to the table, to the hands, to whatever is immediately present. Fragranced with raw beeswax, cedar bark, and crushed bay leaf.
320g · 65hr burn · Beeswax · Antwerp
Still · Type I
VETIVER
A variant of the Still typology with an extended wick and slightly softer beeswax formulation, producing a fractionally larger flame at the same unwavering quality. The fragrance — vetiver root, sandalwood, and a trace of black pepper — was developed specifically for the bedroom: intimate, serious, and quietly warm without being sweet.
180g · 42hr burn · Beeswax · Antwerp
Complex · Type IV
AETHER
Our most technically demanding candle. A multi-wick pour designed to produce a flame that shifts between still and dancing as the wax pool deepens — a behaviour that emerges from the wax geometry rather than external air movement. The effect is that the room seems to breathe. Fragranced with white musk, cardamom, and cold linen.
480g · 80hr burn · Coconut blend · Antwerp

What We Pour With

Materials. No Compromises.

Every material choice at IGNIS begins with the question: does this improve the flame? If the answer is no, the material does not enter the atelier. We do not source by price. We source by performance.

European Beeswax
Sourced from three Belgian apiaries with full traceability. Beeswax burns cleaner than any other natural wax — longer and with a more stable flame than coconut or soy. All Still-typology candles are pure beeswax.
Coconut Wax Blend
Used in Dancing and Complex-typology candles where we need a more responsive flame. The blend is 80% coconut wax and 20% beeswax — a formulation that took four months to calibrate. No paraffin. No synthetic hardeners.
Cotton Core Wicks
Flat-braided, unbleached. The wick diameter and braid structure determine flame height and movement more than any other single variable. We test every new wick batch before it enters production. A wick that performs differently from specification is returned.
Natural Fragrance Bases
All IGNIS fragrances are built from natural fragrance bases — essential oils, absolutes, and resins. No synthetic fragrance chemicals. The concentration is kept low enough that the wax scent of the beeswax remains present. We consider the wax smell part of the fragrance.

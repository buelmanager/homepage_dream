The Holt Legacy

Thomas Holt arrived in Devon from Bristol in 1885 with a lifelong knowledge of maritime rope and a conviction that the industrial age had made craftsmen lazy. He established Cordage & Co. two years later with five rope-makers, a Victorian rope walk, and an absolute prohibition on synthetic materials that holds to this day.

What Thomas could not have anticipated was that his insistence on craft would become, over the following century, not merely a philosophy but a competitive advantage. The rope-makers trained at Cordage & Co. are known throughout maritime and architectural circles as the finest in Britain.

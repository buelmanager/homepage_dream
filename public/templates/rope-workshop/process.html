The Craft

From Fibre to Form

Five Stages

The Commission Process

Stage One
Consultation

Every commission begins with a conversation about purpose, load, environment, and aesthetic requirements. We do not proceed without a full brief.

Stage Two
Fibre Selection

Margaret Carne selects the appropriate natural fibre — manila, hemp, sisal, jute, linen, coir, or cotton — based on the commission requirements. No synthetics are offered.

Stage Three
Laying the Walk

Strands are twisted on our 312-metre Victorian rope walk under constant observation. The tension is adjusted by hand throughout. This stage cannot be rushed.

Stage Four
Hand Testing

Every metre is tested by feel by a master rope-maker. Inconsistencies in tension or strand lay are caught and corrected before the rope leaves the walk.

Stage Five
Delivery & Certificate

Delivered on oak reels with a handwritten commission certificate noting date, fibre, dimension, maker's name, and commission reference.

The Cellar Standard

Every Batch Earns Its Release

No Aurelum batch is released before it is ready. The minimum aging period is 12 months — but some expressions spend two years in the cellar before Marco considers them complete. There is no external pressure on the release schedule.

Every batch is tasted blind against the archive before bottling. If a batch does not meet the standard set by the previous year's equivalent expression, it is held and given additional time. Aurelum has never released a batch that was not ready.

The cellar was built in 1887. It has never been modernised. The temperature has not varied by more than two degrees in a decade. The stone walls are the instrument. We are simply the caretakers.

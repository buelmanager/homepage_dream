The Expressions

One Estate, Three Expressions.

The Athenaion grove is harvested in a single 14-day window each October. From that single harvest, we produce three expressions, differentiated only by the moment of picking within the window — early, peak, and late. Each captures a different profile from the same trees, the same soil, and the same season. No blending between expressions. No addition of other oils. The variety is produced by timing alone.

2025 Harvest · Peloponnese
Early Harvest · October 15–17
Agros — The Field
Harvested at the opening of the window, when the olives are still deep green and the oil yield is at its lowest. Intensely grassy, with sharp pepper on the finish and a pronounced bitterness that mellows over 30 minutes. For those who want the most extreme expression of the Koroneiki character.
500ml · High Phenolic · Limited 200 bottles
Peak Harvest · October 20–22
Petra — The Stone
The centrepiece of the Athenaion range. Harvested at peak phenolic concentration — green-gold in colour, balanced between the bitterness of early harvest and the roundness that comes later. Artichoke, fresh herb, and a mineral finish that reflects the limestone geology of the grove. This is the expression we are best known for.
500ml · Signature Expression · 800 bottles
Late Harvest · October 26–28
Chrysos — The Gold
The final picking of the season, when the fruit begins to turn from green to yellow-gold. The oil is softer, rounder, and more buttery than the earlier expressions. Lower in polyphenols but warmer and more approachable. Almond, ripe apple, and a long smooth finish. Exceptional for raw application over simple dishes.
500ml · Approachable · 400 bottles
Collector's Allocation · Full Season
Tria — The Three
A wooden case containing one bottle of each of the three seasonal expressions, presented with harvest documentation including the pressing date, analysis report, and estate notes for the season. For collectors and those who wish to taste the progression of the harvest window.
3 × 500ml · Numbered Case · 80 sets
Estate Reserve · Select Trees
Palaios — The Ancient
Oil pressed exclusively from the forty trees that remain from the original 1724 planting. The yield is extraordinarily small — fewer than 60 bottles annually. The flavour is unlike any other expression: deeper, earthier, with a resinous quality that comes from three centuries of root development in the same soil. Reserved for longstanding correspondence only.
250ml · Ancient Trees · Under 60 bottles
Gift Edition · Annual Release
Doron — The Gift
The Petra expression in a ceramic vessel commissioned annually from a different Greek ceramicist, in collaboration with the estate. Each year's vessel design is informed by a different element of Peloponnesian culture — this year, a textured surface referencing the limestone formations of the Taygetos range. Includes certificate of authenticity.
500ml Ceramic · Numbered · 120 pieces
Quality Standards

Measured. Documented. Uncompromised.

Every harvest undergoes independent chemical analysis at a certified Athens laboratory before release. We publish the full analysis report for each expression alongside the relevant bottle. There is nothing to hide.

The figures below are drawn from the 2024 Petra expression and are representative of the peak harvest profile. Early harvest figures will differ: higher polyphenols, lower yield, more bitter character.

Free Acidity (FFA)0.12% (max 0.8% for extra virgin)
Peroxide Value4.2 meq O₂/kg
Total Polyphenols612 mg/kg (Petra) · 890+ (Agros)
Extraction MethodCold press, 27°C or below
Harvest to PressUnder 8 hours

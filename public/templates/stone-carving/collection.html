— Stone Selection

The Stone Chooses the Work

Every commission begins at the quarry. We do not select stone from a yard or catalogue — we visit Carrara, Lasa, and Sardinia personally, selecting each block based on its geological personality, crystalline density, and internal veining.

The stone is not raw material. It is a collaborator. Its grain determines the direction of the chisel. Its translucency at depth determines whether a portrait will carry inner light. Its density determines whether a garden piece will outlast the family that commissioned it.

We have rejected quarry visits where no suitable stone was found. We will wait for the right block before we begin.

— Commission

Petra Soma accepts 4–6 new commissions per year. Each begins with a written correspondence. No forms. No telephone. A letter explaining what you wish to create and why it should be made in stone.

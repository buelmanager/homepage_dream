The Roastery

// manifesto.txt

"We believe precision is not the enemy of passion. It is what passion looks like when it has time to mature."

MERIDIAN was founded on a single observation: that the specialty coffee industry — for all its enthusiasm — had a transparency problem. Scores and origins were claimed; the data behind them was rarely shared. We decided to build differently, publishing every cupping score, every roast curve data summary, and every producer relationship in full.

What we discovered in nine years of radical openness is that transparency does not reduce competitive advantage — it increases it. When you have nothing to hide, you roast better. When your producers know you'll publish their data, they perform better. Honesty, it turns out, is a competitive moat.

// founder.md

Magnus Voss

"I came to coffee from chemistry. I left chemistry because coffee was more interesting. The variables are the same — I just care more about the result."

Magnus Voss studied organic chemistry at the University of Copenhagen before joining the specialty coffee industry as a barista, then a roaster, then a trainer. He won the Nordic Roasting Championship in 2011 and 2013 before founding MERIDIAN in 2014 with a simple ambition: to roast coffee with the same rigour he'd learned in the laboratory.

Today he leads a team of six roasters and three sourcing specialists who collectively make over 40 annual origin trips. Magnus oversees every production profile personally before approving it for shipping.

// core_values.md

What We
Stand For

// 01

Radical Transparency

Every cupping score, sourcing relationship, and roast data summary is published openly. We score below 88 — it goes into the reject report, also published.

// 02

Producer First

We price pre-harvest, pay above FLO fair trade minimums in every origin, and return to every farm annually to evaluate progress and share our cup data.

// 03

Science, Not Dogma

We publish our brew parameters but update them per batch. Coffee changes. So do our recommendations. The evidence always wins over the house style.

Nine Years of
Open Data

2014

Founded in Copenhagen

MERIDIAN opens with one Loring roaster and three origins, publishing all cupping scores from day one.

2017

Direct Trade Established

First pre-harvest pricing agreements signed with two Ethiopian and one Colombian farm — pioneering in the Nordic market.

2020

Roast Analytics Platform

In-house software logs 12,000 data points per session and surfaces roast curve anomalies for review within 2 hours of completion.

2024

International Wholesale

60 wholesale accounts across London and New York, all vetted for alignment with MERIDIAN's precision extraction standards.

Savoir-Faire

The Craft

Our Approach

"Perfumery is not chemistry. It is memory made tangible — and that requires patience no formula can abbreviate."

The SÈVE method is built on a simple, radical idea: that no step in the creation of a fragrance can be accelerated without diminishing the result. From the sourcing of raw materials to the final cold-pressing of your bespoke commission, every action is deliberate, measured, and guided by a profound respect for the living materials in our hands.

The Method

Five Movements,
One Signature

Step 01

Deep Consultation

A private two-hour session in our atelier. Through structured olfactory exploration and open conversation, we map the emotional and sensory landscape that will anchor your commission. We record not words, but sensations.

Step 02

Material Curation

Our master perfumer selects from over 340 raw materials, evaluating each one in relation to your brief. This selection is itself a creative act — the canvas before the first brushstroke.

Step 03

Accord Development

Three to five preliminary accords are constructed, macerated for a minimum of eight weeks, and evaluated across different times of day and different seasons. A great fragrance must perform in all conditions.

Step 04

Collaborative Refinement

You return to evaluate each accord on your skin — fragrance is always personal. Your intuitive responses guide successive iterations. This dialogue can span several months, sometimes longer.

Step 05

Final Composition & Bottling

Your fragrance undergoes a final twelve-week maceration before cold-filtration and hand-bottling in bespoke Murano glass. A sealed certificate confirms your exclusive formula. It will never be reproduced.

Raw Materials

A World of
Rare Ingredients

Assam Oud

Origin — Assam, India

Aged a minimum of 40 years in heartwood. Our Assam oud is sourced from a single family estate and evaluated annually before purchase.

Grasse Rose Absolute

Origin — Grasse, France

Cold-extracted from Rosa centifolia at dawn harvest. We accept only the first pressing, discarding what lesser houses consider acceptable.

Aged Ambergris

Origin — Pacific Ocean, collected ethically

Naturally oceanic ambergris aged for decades before use. Its transformation over time creates fixative qualities unmatched by synthetic alternatives.

Iris Absolute

Origin — Florence, Italy

Iris roots are harvested, dried for three years, then steam-distilled to yield an absolute of extraordinary depth — powdery, woody, and ineffably intimate.

# Aurea Mellis — Raw Honey Luxury Landing Page

**Brand:** Aurea Mellis (fictional)
**Tagline:** Liquid Gold From the Wild Hive
**Industry:** Artisan / Single-Origin Raw Honey
**Hero Type:** Type B — Parallax Layers (background-attachment: fixed)

## Brand Identity

Aurea Mellis is a fictional ultra-premium single-origin raw honey atelier based in the Provençal foothills of southern France. Founded by a fictional master apiarist named Édouard Varin, the brand is built around uncompromising cold extraction, botanical terroir mapping, and radical transparency in batch documentation.

**Color Palette:**
- Background: `#1E1A14` (deep warm charcoal)
- Surface: `#2A2318` (dark amber-brown)
- Gold accent: `#C9A84C` (warm harvest gold)
- Cream text: `#F0E8D8`
- Muted: `#8A7D68`

**Typography:**
- Display: Cormorant Garamond (luxury serif)
- Titles: Cinzel (classical Roman caps)
- Body: Inter (clean sans-serif)

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about chartering with AZUR YACHTS.

What is included in the charter price?

+
Our charter price includes the yacht, professional crew (captain, chef, stewardess), fuel for standard cruising routes, port fees at scheduled stops, all onboard meals and beverages, water toys, and comprehensive insurance. Additional costs may apply for special requests such as helicopter transfers, spa therapists, or premium wine selections beyond the standard offering.

Do I need sailing experience to charter a yacht?

+
Absolutely not. All our charters come with a fully licensed professional crew who handle every aspect of navigation and vessel operation. Your only job is to relax and enjoy the experience. However, if you are an experienced sailor and wish to take the helm, we offer bareboat charter options for certified captains with appropriate licenses.

How far in advance should I book a charter?

+
We recommend booking at least 3 to 6 months in advance, especially for peak season (June through September in the Mediterranean, December through March in the Caribbean). Popular yachts and prime dates can book up to a year ahead. However, we sometimes have last-minute availability, so do not hesitate to inquire about shorter notice bookings.

Can the itinerary be customized?

+
Every AZUR YACHTS voyage is fully customizable. Our charter specialists work with you to design the perfect route based on your interests, whether that is secluded beaches, vibrant nightlife ports, historic coastal towns, or world-class diving spots. The itinerary remains flexible during the voyage as well, so you can change plans based on weather, mood, or spontaneous discoveries.

What happens in case of bad weather?

+
Safety is our absolute priority. Our captains monitor weather conditions continuously and will adjust the itinerary to ensure comfortable and safe cruising. In rare cases of severe weather, we will anchor in a protected bay or remain in port. Our yachts are equipped with stabilizers for comfort in moderate conditions. If significant weather disruptions affect your charter, we work with clients on flexible rescheduling options.

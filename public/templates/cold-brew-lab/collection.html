The Spectrum

Six Extractions. One Temperature.

Every bottle in the CRYO LAB catalogue begins at 2°C and ends 22 hours later. What differs is the origin, the roast profile, and the extraction ratio. Each brew is a precisely controlled variable within a fixed protocol. We do not blend. We do not adjust for palatability. We document what the bean becomes when treated as a specimen.

Sourcing Protocol

Every Origin Documented. Every Batch Numbered.

We source from fewer than twelve farms annually. Each relationship is built over multiple seasons before a single gram reaches our extraction chamber. Farm elevation, varietal, processing method, and harvest date are recorded for every batch.

Quality for CRYO LAB is not a marketing position. It is a technical baseline. If an origin does not perform within expected parameters at 2°C, it does not enter the catalogue.

Extraction Temperature 2°C ± 0.5°C
Standard Duration 22 Hours
Grind Precision ± 3 microns
Batch Size Max 200 litres
Shelf Life (sealed) 14 days

Questions

Frequently Asked

What membership options are available?

We offer three tiers: Gold ($150/month) includes lounge access and one complimentary cigar per visit. Platinum ($350/month) adds private humidor rental, priority reservations, and invitations to exclusive tasting events. Black ($750/month) is our most elite tier with unlimited access, a personal cigar concierge, and VIP event seating. Annual memberships receive two months free.

Is there a dress code?

We maintain a smart-casual dress code to preserve the refined atmosphere our members expect. Collared shirts, dress shoes, and tailored attire are encouraged. Athletic wear, flip-flops, and excessively casual clothing are not permitted. We want every guest to feel the elegance of the space.

How does cigar storage work?

Our private humidors are maintained at a precise 70% humidity and 70 degrees Fahrenheit -- the ideal conditions for cigar preservation. Each humidor is individually locked with biometric access. Platinum and Black members receive complimentary storage. Gold members can add storage for $75/month. We also offer cigar aging services for collectors.

Can I book a private event?

Absolutely. Our private event room accommodates up to 30 guests and can be configured for corporate gatherings, celebrations, or intimate dinners. Packages include a dedicated event coordinator, curated cigar menu, full bar service, and optional catering from our partner restaurants. Contact us at least two weeks in advance for availability.

What is the age requirement?

All guests must be 21 years of age or older. Valid government-issued photo identification is required upon entry. This policy is strictly enforced to comply with New York state law and to maintain the exclusive adult atmosphere that defines the Oak & Smoke experience.

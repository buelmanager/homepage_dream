Origin

From Tradition,
Not Trend

Caelum House was founded by Dr. Helena Voss in Zürich in 1998, following a decade of postgraduate study in the history of astrological practice at the University of Geneva and a three-year apprenticeship under the late Viennese astrologer Franz Kellner.

Her guiding conviction was and remains this: astrology practised with intellectual rigour and psychological depth is among the most powerful instruments of self-knowledge available. It is not entertainment. It is not prediction. It is a language — one worth learning to speak with precision.

Caelum House now operates from three practice locations — Zürich, London, and New York — with a team of six astrologers, each trained to the house standard of classical method and contemporary psychological integration.

What We Stand For

Core Values

☀
Rigour

Every reading at Caelum House is grounded in classical technique — Hellenistic, medieval, and modern synthesis. We do not offer pop astrology. We offer a serious, trained interpretation of a complex symbolic system.

◆
Discretion

A consultation at Caelum House is a private engagement. Client data is never shared, never used for marketing, and retained only as long as the client requests. Our discretion is unconditional.

∞
Depth

We do not offer ten-minute readings. Our minimum appointment is ninety minutes. We believe that a natal chart explored with care over time is worth more than a hundred charts skimmed in succession.

History

A Practice Built Over Decades

1998
Foundation

Dr. Helena Voss opens Caelum House in the Altstadt district of Zürich, practising natal chart consultations by appointment for a small private clientele.

2006
London Practice Opens

The Mayfair practice opens, with Dr. Voss travelling between Zürich and London quarterly. Caelum House expands its team to include two additional practitioners.

2015
The Caelum Method

A formal training programme is established for Caelum House practitioners — a two-year supervised apprenticeship combining classical technique with contemporary psychological frameworks.

2022
New York Consultation Rooms

The Manhattan practice opens on the Upper East Side, completing the three-city network and making Caelum House accessible across three time zones.

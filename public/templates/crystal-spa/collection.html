The Collection

Every treatment is a singular protocol

No two sessions at LUMARA are identical. Each treatment below represents a category of practice — a set of crystals, techniques, and intentions — from which your practitioner will compose your individual session following bioenergetic assessment. What you receive is always your own.

LUMARA operates six core treatment categories, each anchored in a primary mineral and informed by decades of practice. Our treatments range from 90-minute introductory sessions to the three-hour Grand Ritual, which remains the definitive LUMARA experience and requires a separate consultation to book.

Duration, depth, and the specific crystals selected for your session are determined on the day. We invite you to arrive with openness and no fixed expectations. The mineral will meet you where you are.

Mineral Standards

The crystals we choose to work with

LUMARA's mineral collection consists of 2,300 individual specimens, each sourced directly from its country of origin and assessed for piezoelectric activity before entering our practice. We maintain active relationships with twelve specialist mineral suppliers across six continents.

Amethyst — Uruguay & Zambia

Our primary working stone. We source cathedral-grade amethyst from two mines — one in Artigas, Uruguay, one in the Copperbelt of Zambia. Each batch is tested for piezoelectric output before acceptance.

Selenite — Morocco & Oklahoma

We work with two distinct selenite formations: fine-grain Moroccan slabs for structural treatments, and optically clear Oklahoma gypsum for facial and precision work. Both are cut and polished in-house.

Rose Quartz — Madagascar

Our rose quartz spheres are carved from a single Malagasy source whose particular translucency and internal structure we have worked with since our opening year. We do not substitute.

Black Tourmaline — Brazil

Used as a grounding anchor in every treatment regardless of category. Our Paraiba-sourced tourmaline carries a consistent negative electrical charge that provides the foundation for the resonance work above it.

How We Work

The Ritual

The Process

A ritual built on listening

Every LUMARA treatment follows the same five-stage ritual — not because we believe in rigid protocols, but because we have learned that the body needs time to open. The stages create a container of trust within which the real work becomes possible.

What varies is everything within each stage: the minerals selected, the techniques employed, the amount of time given to each phase. These are determined on the day, by your practitioner, in response to who you are when you arrive.

Five Stages

The LUMARA ritual sequence

I
Arrival — 20 minutes

Bioenergetic Assessment

You arrive and are received not at a reception desk but in the Mineral Room — a space lined with 400 specimens arranged by geological period. Your practitioner greets you here and begins the session immediately: a silent twenty-minute bioenergetic assessment in which they read your field using a trio of obsidian dowsing stones. You are seated, fully clothed, and asked to do nothing except be present. The assessment determines everything that follows.

II
Selection — 15 minutes

Crystal Protocol Design

Your practitioner withdraws to our preparation room — a space you do not enter — and selects the specific minerals for your session from our collection. This selection is guided by the assessment and by a proprietary compatibility matrix developed over nine years of practice. You may be asked three or four questions. You will not be asked to choose your own crystals; that is our work, not yours. When the selection is complete, you are invited to the treatment space.

III
Treatment — 60–150 minutes

The Crystal Session

The treatment itself. Duration varies by category — from 60 minutes for a Crystal Consultation to three hours for the Grand Ritual. You lie on a heated treatment table in the semi-dark. Your practitioner places the selected crystals at specific points along your body, works the peripheral field with directional wands, and may employ sound — crystal singing bowls, Tibetan bells — where the assessment indicates acoustic resonance will deepen the work. We do not speak during this phase unless you initiate.

IV
Integration — 20 minutes

The Settling

The crystals are removed slowly, in reverse order of placement. You remain on the table for twenty minutes while your practitioner is present but silent. This integration period is not optional; it is part of the treatment. The body processes during stillness in ways it cannot during movement. You are offered water infused with clear quartz and a small ceramic bowl of mineral-enriched salt to hold — the weight and temperature of these objects assist the nervous system in completing its return to ordinary wakefulness.

V
Debrief — 15 minutes

Session Notes & Prescription

When you are ready, you and your practitioner sit together for a fifteen-minute debrief. They share their observations from the session, document any notable responses to particular minerals, and provide a written prescription — a handwritten card, sealed with wax — that specifies the treatment category and mineral sequence most beneficial for your next visit. You leave with this card and with a small parting mineral — a tumbled stone selected specifically for you — to carry until your next appointment.

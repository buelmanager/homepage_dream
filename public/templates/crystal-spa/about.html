Founder & Origin

Born from a decade of mineral study

LUMARA was founded in London's Knightsbridge in 2014 by Dr. Seren Aytaç, a crystallographer who spent a decade studying piezoelectric properties of quartz at the University of Cambridge before concluding that the most interesting application of her research was not industrial but human.

She opened LUMARA with a collection of forty-seven minerals sourced from six continents and a team of three therapists trained in bioenergetic assessment. A decade later, the team is seventeen, the mineral collection is 2,300 specimens, and the waiting list for first appointments is eight weeks.

Our Philosophy

Three principles that govern every treatment

◆
Mineral Integrity

Every crystal used in a LUMARA treatment is sourced directly from its country of origin, assessed for piezoelectric activity, and rejected if it falls below our conductivity threshold. We do not use synthetic crystals. We do not use crystals we cannot trace.

◇
Individual Assessment

No two LUMARA clients receive the same treatment. Every session begins with a bioenergetic assessment — a 20-minute reading of the client's energetic field — before a crystal protocol is selected. We have never offered a menu of fixed treatments. We never will.

◈
Scientific Foundation

LUMARA's protocols are developed in collaboration with our scientific advisory board — a team of physicists, biologists, and sound researchers who help us understand what our crystals are doing and why. We make no claims we cannot support. We acknowledge much that we do not yet understand.

Hide Selection

Leather is the art, not the medium

We source from three tanneries only — each visited annually, each known to us by name and by hand. We reject any hide that does not meet our grain and temper standards, regardless of cost.

Conceria Walpier — Tuscany

Our primary source since 1991. Vegetable-tanned bridle leather with exceptional firmness and an extraordinary patina trajectory over 20+ years.

Tärnsjö Garveri — Sweden

Swedish vegetable-tanned leather from a family tannery founded in 1873. Our domestic source, used for pieces where a lighter, more supple temper is required.

Thomas Ware — England

English bridle leather for belt straps and structural applications. The heaviest hide we use — 4–5mm tempered butt leather, edge-burnished before cutting.

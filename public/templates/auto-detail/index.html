FAQ

Common questions about detailing.

How long does detailing take?

+
Essential package takes 4-6 hours, Correction package takes 1-2 days, Full Protection package takes 3-5 days. Time may vary based on vehicle condition and size. We'll provide accurate timing after vehicle inspection.

What's the difference between ceramic coating and wax?

+
Wax provides temporary protection lasting 1-3 months, while ceramic coating is semi-permanent lasting 3-5 years. Ceramic coating forms a 9H hardness glass-like layer that protects paint from UV, acid rain, and contaminants, providing self-cleaning effect and deep gloss.

When is PPF (Paint Protection Film) needed?

+
PPF is a transparent film that protects paint from physical impacts (stone chips, scratches, door dings). Recommended for new cars, high-value vehicles, or those planning long-term ownership. Has self-healing properties for light scratches.

Can I visit without a reservation?

+
Detailing is by appointment only. Please reserve online or by phone for vehicle inspection and accurate quote. Same-day reservations may be possible but waiting may occur.

Is there a waiting area?

+
Yes, we have a dedicated customer lounge with Wi-Fi, coffee, magazines. You can watch the work through glass windows. For long procedures, we offer vehicle pickup/delivery service (Seoul area only).

WHY NEON WORKS

UV-reactive inks are a fundamentally different medium from conventional tattoo pigment. They behave differently under different light conditions, age differently, and require different technical approaches. Most tattoo artists avoid them because of the complexity. We built our entire practice around mastering them.

At Voltage Ink, every artist spends a minimum of three months learning UV ink behaviour before they use it on a client. We test every batch of ink on synthetic skin before it enters the studio.

  • EU-certified inks tested for heavy metals and allergens
  • UV-reactive inks from labs with published long-term safety data
  • Patch testing offered for all UV-reactive work
  • All artists trained specifically in neon pigment layering
  • Aftercare protocol includes UV protection guidance
  • 12-month complimentary touch-up guarantee on all work

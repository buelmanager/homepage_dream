## Design Concept

**Style:** Dark luxury with warm gold accents — evokes high-end jewelry stores and exclusive ateliers.

**Color Palette:**
- Background: `#0A0810` (Deep midnight)
- Surface: `#13101E` (Dark aubergine)
- Accent: `#C9A96E` (Warm antique gold)
- Accent Dark: `#A8864F`
- Text: `#F0EDE8` (Warm ivory)
- Text Muted: `#7A7568`
- Border: `#2A2535`

**Typography:**
- Headings: `Cormorant Garamond` — editorial elegance, timeless serif
- Body: `Jost` — clean, modern, refined

**Key Differentiators:**
- Ring/diamond SVG preloader instead of building wireframe
- Gold shimmer effect on section titles
- Gem-cut angle motifs in corner decorations
- Velvet-feel card backgrounds with subtle grain
- Jewel-tone image treatment (warm, rich saturation)

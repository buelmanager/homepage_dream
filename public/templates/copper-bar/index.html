Material Process

The Craft

01 / 03
The Vessel

Each cocktail is served in a hand-hammered copper cup sourced from a single artisan atelier in Istanbul. Patina is earned, never applied.

02 / 03
The Alchemy

Distillates are selected by oxidation potential — how they will interact with copper ions over time. Every formula is a material hypothesis.

03 / 03
The Origin

Copper sourced from three provinces: Anatolia, Extremadura, and Yunnan. Each region lends a different mineral character to the reaction.

Five Stages

Atelier Process

Stage 01
Raw Selection

Distillates chosen for copper-reactive potential. Tasted blind, sourced anonymous.

Stage 02
Reduction

Base spirits reduced over copper alembic at 62°C for eighteen minutes.

Stage 03
Blending

Ratios calculated by reaction potential, not flavour alone. Science first.

Stage 04
Temperature

Final liquid held at 4°C for 24 hours in copper chamber before service.

Stage 05
Service

Presented in numbered copper vessels. Each piece unique. Each reaction singular.

Textile Philosophy

The cloth before the garment

KANADE works only with textiles produced within a ten-kilometre radius of our Kyoto atelier, or sourced from a small number of trusted mills we have worked with for decades.

Nishijin-ori

The woven textile that defines Kyoto luxury. Complex Jacquard patterns woven at 3cm per hour on heritage looms.

Chirimen Crepe

A silk crepe ground with distinctive texture. Our chirimen is sourced from Tango Peninsula weavers who supply no more than six ateliers worldwide.

Habutae Silk

The smooth silk ground used for hand-painted tsujigahana work. Our habutae is weighted with persimmon tannin, a process unchanged since 1890.

## What is HomeDream?

HomeDream is a platform offering luxury, beautifully crafted homepage templates. Browse stylish designs across a wide range of categories, preview them instantly in your browser, and download them to use in your projects.

## Getting Started with Free Templates

1. **Sign up** — Create an account quickly with your Google or GitHub credentials
2. **Browse templates** — Explore a variety of designs organized by category
3. **Preview** — See how each template looks across desktop, tablet, and mobile screens
4. **Download** — Grab the template that fits your vision

## FREE vs PRO

- **FREE** — Download any free-tier template with no limits
- **PRO** — $20/month for unlimited downloads of all PRO templates, including a commercial license

## Tips for Using Templates

> Templates are a starting point. Customize them with your own brand colors and content to make them truly yours.

- Brand colors can be updated easily using CSS variables
- Swap out images and copy to complete your homepage
- All templates are fully responsive and work seamlessly on mobile devices
